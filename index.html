Welcome to KatanaHeiroll

At KatanaHeiroll, we bring the authentic taste of Japan to your table. Our culinary team, led by Master Chef Heiroll Magcalas with over 22 years of experience, crafts each dish with precision, passion, and the freshest ingredients.

What sets us apart is our commitment to traditional Japanese cooking techniques combined with innovative culinary artistry. Each plate is a harmonious balance of flavors, textures, and visual appeal, honoring the Japanese concept of "washoku" – harmony of food.

Founded in 2010, KatanaHeiroll began as a small family restaurant with a dream to share the rich culinary heritage of Japan. Today, we've grown to become a beloved destination for Japanese cuisine enthusiasts while maintaining our dedication to authenticity and excellence.

Our best-selling dishes include our meticulously prepared Signature Omakase Sushi Platter and our delicate yet flavorful Wagyu Beef Sukiyaki, dishes that have earned us recognition among food critics and loyal customers alike.
